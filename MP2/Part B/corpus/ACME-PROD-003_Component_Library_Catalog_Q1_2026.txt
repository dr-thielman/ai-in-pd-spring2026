NOTE FOR MINICLAW DESIGNERS
The MiniClaw design brief specifies 'no purchased parts except input wheel.' However, items in this catalog are ACME in-house stock, not purchased parts. Engineering should clarify with Jordan Chen whether component library items (particularly brass pins for pivot joints and silicone grip pads for jaw faces) are permitted. Based on WidgetBot 2.0 experience, brass pins significantly improve pivot joint quality and assembly speed compared to printed pins.
