QUALITY CONTROL
Quality sampling rate: 1 in 50 units gets a full function test. Full function test includes:
- Jaw opening and closing through full range of motion
- Force check: minimum 5 N grip force at 25 mm opening
- Visual inspection: no visible layer defects, stringing, or color inconsistency on exterior surfaces
- Logo readability check
Reject criteria: gears that bind or skip, jaws misaligned by more than 1 mm, visible print defects on exterior surfaces, thumb wheel that is too loose or too tight to turn comfortably.
We will print 50 extra units (550 total) to account for rejects.
