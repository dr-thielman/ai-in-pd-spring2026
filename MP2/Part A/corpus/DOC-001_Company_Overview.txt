Ridgeline Engineering Partners is a mechanical engineering consultancy headquartered in Portland, Oregon. The firm was founded in 2007 by Dr. Catherine Marsh and David Okonkwo, both former senior engineers at Bechtel, who saw an opportunity to provide specialized structural and thermal analysis services to mid-market clients who could not justify full-time engineering staff for niche technical problems.

Over the past eighteen years, the firm has grown from a two-person partnership to a consultancy employing 138 engineers and technical staff across three offices. Ridgeline's core competencies span structural analysis, thermal management, mechanical design, and prototype development. The firm serves clients in transportation infrastructure, consumer electronics, HVAC systems, and medical devices. Annual revenue for fiscal year 2024 was approximately $42 million, representing a 12% increase over the prior year.

Ridgeline's mission statement, adopted in 2015, reads: 'We deliver rigorous engineering analysis that helps our clients build safer, more efficient, and more reliable products and infrastructure.' The firm holds ISO 9001:2015 certification and maintains professional liability insurance through Hartford Engineering Underwriters. Key long-term clients include Millbrook County Department of Transportation, Cascade Consumer Products, and NovaMed Devices.

Dr. Marsh currently serves as Managing Partner and oversees the Portland and Denver offices. David Okonkwo leads the firm's business development efforts and manages the Seattle office. The firm's technical advisory board includes Dr. Patricia Nguyen (Stanford, computational mechanics), Dr. James Whitfield (Georgia Tech, thermal sciences), and Professor Rachel Kim (University of Washington, biomedical engineering).
