RIDGELINE ENGINEERING PARTNERS — DESIGN REVIEW CHECKLIST
Form Number: ENG-DR-01, Revision 7
Effective Date: June 2024
Maintained by: Brian Tanaka, PE, Director of Engineering

This checklist is required for all design deliverables before client submission. The reviewing engineer must be a Senior Engineer I or above and must not have been involved in the original design work.

GENERAL REQUIREMENTS
[ ] Project number and title correctly identified on all deliverables
[ ] Client name and contact verified
[ ] Applicable codes and standards identified and correct edition used
[ ] Design basis document reviewed and requirements traced
[ ] All assumptions documented and justified
[ ] Units consistent throughout (SI or US Customary, not mixed)

ANALYSIS VERIFICATION
[ ] Input data verified against source documents
[ ] Boundary conditions reviewed for appropriateness
[ ] Material properties sourced from approved references
[ ] Load combinations per applicable code
[ ] Independent hand calculation performed for critical results
[ ] FEA mesh convergence study documented (if applicable)
[ ] Results checked for physical reasonableness
[ ] Safety factors or rating factors reported and assessed

DRAWINGS AND MODELS
[ ] GD&T per ASME Y14.5-2018
[ ] Tolerances achievable by specified manufacturing process
[ ] Material specifications called out correctly
[ ] Surface finish requirements specified where needed
[ ] All views necessary for fabrication included
[ ] CAD model matches drawings (no undocumented changes)

REPORT QUALITY
[ ] Executive summary present and accurate
[ ] Methodology clearly described
[ ] All figures and tables numbered and captioned
[ ] References complete and properly cited
[ ] Conclusions supported by analysis results
[ ] Recommendations actionable and prioritized
[ ] Spell check and grammar review completed
