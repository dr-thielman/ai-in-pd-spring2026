RIDGELINE ENGINEERING PARTNERS — TECHNICAL STANDARDS GUIDE
Revision 12, Updated September 2024
Maintained by: Lisa Fernandez, PE, Director of Quality

This document lists the primary technical standards referenced across Ridgeline's practice areas. Engineers must use the editions listed below unless a client contract specifies otherwise. Copies of all listed standards are available in the firm's digital standards library (SharePoint > Technical Resources > Standards Library).

STRUCTURAL ANALYSIS
- AASHTO LRFD Bridge Design Specifications, 9th Edition (2020)
- AASHTO Manual for Bridge Evaluation, 3rd Edition (2018)
- AISC 360-22: Specification for Structural Steel Buildings
- ACI 318-19: Building Code Requirements for Structural Concrete
- ASCE 7-22: Minimum Design Loads for Buildings
- AWS D1.1/D1.1M-2020: Structural Welding Code — Steel

THERMAL AND FLUID ANALYSIS
- ASHRAE Standard 90.1-2019: Energy Standard for Buildings
- ASHRAE Handbook — Fundamentals (2021 edition)
- IPC-2152: Standard for Determining Current-Carrying Capacity in PCBs
- JEDEC JESD51-14: Transient Dual Interface Test Method

MECHANICAL DESIGN
- ASME Y14.5-2018: Dimensioning and Tolerancing
- ASME Y14.100-2017: Engineering Drawing Practices
- ISO 2768-1:1989: General Tolerances (linear and angular)
- ISO 286-1:2010: ISO System of Limits and Fits

MEDICAL DEVICES
- ISO 13485:2016: Quality Management Systems for Medical Devices
- ISO 14971:2019: Application of Risk Management to Medical Devices
- IEC 60601-1: Medical Electrical Equipment — General Requirements
- ISO 10993 series: Biological Evaluation of Medical Devices

QUALITY AND TESTING
- ISO 9001:2015: Quality Management Systems — Requirements
- ASTM E8/E8M: Standard Test Methods for Tension Testing of Metals
- ASTM D638: Standard Test Method for Tensile Properties of Plastics
- IEC 60529: Degrees of Protection Provided by Enclosures (IP Code)

Engineers are responsible for verifying that the applicable edition of any standard has not been superseded before beginning analysis. The Quality Director distributes quarterly updates on new standard editions.
