INTERNAL MEMO
TO: All Staff
FROM: David Okonkwo, Partner
DATE: January 15, 2025
RE: Seattle Office Update and Growth Plan

I am pleased to share an update on our Seattle operations as we begin the new year. As many of you know, the Seattle office was established to better serve our growing client base in the Puget Sound region, particularly in the aerospace supply chain and medical device sectors.

Our Seattle office opened in 2019 and has grown steadily since then. We currently employ 47 engineers at the Seattle location, spanning structural, thermal, and mechanical design disciplines. The office is located in the Fremont district at 3847 Leary Way NW, occupying the third and fourth floors of the Fremont Engineering Center. Our lease runs through December 2028 with an option to expand into the fifth floor.

Key achievements for the Seattle office in 2024 include the completion of the CardioSense medical device enclosure project for NovaMed Devices, the Puget Sound Energy substation thermal assessment, and the initiation of three new aerospace supplier qualification projects. The office generated $14.2 million in revenue for FY 2024, exceeding our target by 8%.

Looking ahead, we plan to add 10-12 engineers in 2025, with emphasis on hiring in finite element analysis and computational fluid dynamics. We are also evaluating a satellite workspace in Bellevue to accommodate staff who live on the Eastside. I encourage anyone interested in transferring to Seattle to reach out to HR. The cost-of-living adjustment for Seattle-based staff is 7% above the Portland baseline.
