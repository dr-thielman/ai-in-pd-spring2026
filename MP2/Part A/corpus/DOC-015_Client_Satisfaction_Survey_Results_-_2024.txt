RIDGELINE ENGINEERING PARTNERS
CLIENT SATISFACTION SURVEY — 2024 ANNUAL RESULTS
Prepared by: Karen Weatherby, PMP, Director of Project Management
Date: December 2024

METHODOLOGY
The annual client satisfaction survey was distributed to project contacts for all engagements completed between January and October 2024. Of 87 survey invitations sent, 54 responses were received (62% response rate, up from 58% in 2023). The survey uses a 1-5 Likert scale across six dimensions.

RESULTS SUMMARY

Category                        | 2024 Score | 2023 Score | Change
Technical Quality               |    4.6     |    4.5     |  +0.1
Communication & Responsiveness  |    4.3     |    4.1     |  +0.2
Schedule Adherence              |    4.1     |    3.9     |  +0.2
Budget Management               |    4.2     |    4.2     |   0.0
Value for Fee                   |    4.0     |    3.8     |  +0.2
Overall Satisfaction            |    4.4     |    4.2     |  +0.2

Overall satisfaction improved from 4.2 to 4.4 on the 5-point scale. The largest improvements were in Communication & Responsiveness and Value for Fee, both reflecting the firm's investment in project management training during 2024.

KEY THEMES FROM OPEN-ENDED RESPONSES
Positive: Clients consistently praised the depth of technical expertise, particularly in structural and thermal analysis. Several clients specifically mentioned the quality of deliverable reports. Three clients cited Ridgeline's ability to meet aggressive schedules as a differentiator.

Areas for improvement: The most common suggestion (12 of 54 responses) was improving the availability of interim progress updates, particularly for longer engagements. Five respondents requested more flexible billing arrangements, such as monthly fixed-fee retainers instead of hourly billing.

NET PROMOTER SCORE
NPS for 2024: +47 (up from +38 in 2023). 31 promoters, 19 passives, 4 detractors. Industry benchmark for engineering consultancies is +32.
